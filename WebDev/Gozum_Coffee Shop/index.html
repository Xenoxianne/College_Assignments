About Us

Where: B3 L3 Eastridge Village, Bgy San Roque, Angono, Rizal
Store Hours: 1pm - 9pm (Tuesday to Sunday)

Welcome to our cozy café, where delicious aromas and warm ambiance await you. At our café, we take pride in serving freshly brewed coffees, tantalizing teas, and delightful pastries that will satisfy your cravings and awaken your taste buds. Our dedicated team of baristas crafts each cup with care, ensuring every sip is a moment of pure bliss. Whether you're seeking a serene spot to work, catch up with friends, or simply indulge in a moment of solitude, our café provides the perfect retreat. Join us and experience a haven of flavors, friendly smiles, and a place where time slows down, allowing you to savor life's simple pleasures.
